Our Manifesto

The Simple Way to Hunt Stocks

Stock investing is one of the most powerful ways to build wealth. Yet it is still inaccessible to billions worldwide.

We believe stock investing shouldn't be reserved for the few who speak the language of finance. It should be simple so everyone, everywhere can build wealth.

Our promise to our StockPandas

StockPanda was born to demystify stock investing for everyone.

To achieve this we are focusing on six core pillars.

What SIMPLE means to us
S
We keep you Safe.
Your money is not a game. That's why we focus heavily on risk — not fancy charts or hype. We put your financial wellbeing at the centre of everything we do.
I
We go International.
Markets don't stop at borders. Neither do we. From New York to London, Tokyo to Johannesburg — we cover the world's markets so you never miss a hunt.
M
We stay Modern.
AI-powered, mobile-first, and built for the modern era to ensure future generations are not locked out of wealth-building opportunities like their predecessors.
P
We make it Personal.
Your portfolio, your risk appetite, your goals. StockPanda provides the tools so you can filter out the noise and focus on what really matters to you — not everyone.
L
We help you Learn.
Complex concepts, plain language. We don't dumb it down — we open it up. Every metric, every signal, every chart explained so you leave smarter than you arrived.
E
We are Expressive.
Data should be felt, not just read. We use visual cues, colour, and design to turn numbers into narrative — so you can read the market at a glance.
